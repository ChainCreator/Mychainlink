🗑 Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your data. You can delete your My Chain Link account at any time.

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Deleting your account will remove:

  • Your profile and personal information
  • All your posts, photos, and videos
  • All your comments and likes
  • Your messages and connections
  • Your subscription and payment data

How to Delete Your Account:

  1. Sign in to your My Chain Link account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Enter your password to confirm

Your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
