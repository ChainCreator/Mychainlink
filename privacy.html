Privacy Policy

Your data. Your control. No surprises.

Last updated: July 9, 2026

My Chain Link (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website mychainlink.ca and the My Chain Link mobile web application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, username/handle, password, and location (optional). If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email, and profile photo from those services.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to add a bio, profile photo, and location to your profile.
  • Content You Create: Posts, photos, comments, messages, and live stream content you share on the platform.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, features used) to improve our service.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to premium content or connect PayPal for creator monetization, payment processing is handled directly by PayPal. We do not store your full payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the My Chain Link service
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Enable social features (posts, messages, connections, live streams)
  • Process payments for subscriptions and creator earnings
  • Send you important account notifications and security alerts
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information, posts, and public content are visible to other users of the platform according to your privacy settings.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (Supabase for data storage, PayPal for payments, Google/Facebook for authentication) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • Account Control: You can edit your profile, change your password, and update your information at any time in Settings.
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Available in Settings for additional account security.
  • Data Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Settings menu. This action is irreversible.
  • Cookie Preferences: We use minimal cookies for session management and authentication. You can clear cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Row-level security policies on all database tables
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

6. Children's Privacy

My Chain Link is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google — Sign-in and authentication
  • Meta/Facebook — Sign-in and authentication
  • PayPal — Payment processing
  • Supabase — Database and authentication infrastructure

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through the app. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
